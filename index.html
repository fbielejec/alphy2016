How do we do it?

Use Bayesian statistics to model the data

For every $n\geq0$, given time-points $0\leq t_{0}\leq t_{1}<\ldots < t_{n}\leq t_{n+1}$ and discrete states $i_{0},i_{1},\ldots,i_{n},i_{n+1}$, it holds that:
$$P\left\{ X(t_{n+1})=i_{n+1}\mid X(t_{n})=i_{n},\ldots,X(t_{0})=i_{0}\right\}=$$ $$=P\left\{ X(t_{n+1})=i_{n+1}\mid X(t_{n})=i_{n}\right\}$$
